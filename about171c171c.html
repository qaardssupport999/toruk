concept

The Pakistan Amateur Golf League (toruk makto) has been conceptualized as the world's first golf league comprising franchisee-owned teams, with the objectives to:

  • Provide a platform to Amateur Golfers (the largest segment in the world of golf), to play in a professional tournaments like setup with LIVE broadcast, amongst other advantages.
  • Create opportunities for Team Owners, Sponsors, Partners and Players for marketing, branding, networking, customer development, entertainment and public relations.
  • Reinvigorate the spirit of Amateur Golf in a first-of-its-kind competitive team format.
  • Promote Amateur Golf in the PAKISTAN and underline the country's importance as a golfing destination.

vision

"To create value through amateur golf for all stakeholders - Team Owners, Players, Sponsors, Partners and Organizers, and contribute to the promotion of Golf in the PAKISTAN."

promoters

The toruk makto is the brainchild of Mr. Sudesh Aggarwal, a passionate and well-known amateur golfer and Chairman of Giant Group of Companies based in Dubai, supported by his team and a majority of amateur golfers in the region.

The toruk makto shall be managed by Giant Entertainment & Events DMCC, a group event management company.

format of Play

The Pakistan Amateur Golf League (toruk makto) will be contested by eight franchisee-owned teams in round robin league under various match play formats of the game.

There will be seven rounds of league matches, thus ensuring each team plays against the other seven teams. This will be followed by semi-finals and the Finals.

Tournament Format

Each team will play one round of golf against other teams in a Round Robin format. Thus, with all eight teams playing Round 1, there will be 5 franchises with 6 or 12 Matches in each franchises as illustrated below: other teams in a Round Robin format. Thus, with all eight teams playing Round 1, there will be 4 Groups with 6 or 12 Matches in each Group as illustrated below:

  • Group 1 - Team 1 against Team 2 - 6 or 12 Matches
  • Group 2 - Team 3 against Team 4 - 6 or 12 Matches
  • Group 3 - Team 5 against Team 6 - 6 or 12 Matches
  • Group 4 - Team 7 against Team 8 - 6 or 12 Matches

A total of 96 amateur golfers will compete in any given round. This will be repeated for the subsequent rounds.

The matches will be played under one of the following formats

  • Individual Match Play
  • Individual Stroke Play
  • Individual Stableford
  • 2 Ball Team - Better Ball Match Play
  • As decided by Tournament Committee

Point System

Winner of each Match will fetch 2 points for the Team, while the loser gets 0 point. In case of halve, both teams will get one point each.

The above stated format of play may be revised at the discretion of Franchiser
