Toruk Makto GOLF LEAGUE

WHAT IS Toruk makto?

Welcome to a new age of Golf in Franchise League format. Get ready to witness eight teams brimming with talent fiercely competing for top honours at the new Toruk Makto. A League created by Abrar Asif, the Pakistan.

Toruk Makto is a path-breaking initiative by the organizers to bring a new dimension to the lovely sport. Add new aggression. Provide a great platform for amateurs. Place the Pakistan at the centre of the action. And bring to life a play format the world has never seen before. The League presents a great opportunity for Team owners, Sponsors, Partners and Players to network and provide an impetus to brand building, marketing, customer development, entertainment and public relations.

Welcome to Toruk Makto Golf League 2021.

A FANTASTIC PROPOSITION FOR TEAM OWNERS

Toruk Makto is a fantastic opportunity for prospective team owners to own a team of their choice, and strike a win-win partnership with sponsors, amateur players, advertisers, corporates, media and the audience. Curated by Abrar Asif managed by and sanctioned by Toruk Makato, Golf is ready to tee off into a new horizon, and you have a chance to own the exciting new future of the sport.

TOP-OF-MIND QUESTIONS

CAN THE TEAM OWNER CHOOSE THE PLAYERS?

All the Players who intend to play in the League have to be registered and come into Players Pool. The Players can register directly or through the Team Owners. Each Team squad is of 24 Players, 6 from each category as under:

HANDICAP INDEX NO. OF PLAYERS
0-4 6
5-8 6
9-11 6
12-14 6

Team Owners shall be allowed to choose 2 Players from each category and other 4 Players from each category will be by draw. Team Owner shall be allowed to change the Player from its quota at any time after registering the Player and coming in Players Pool.

WHAT IS THE 'LIVE BROADCAST AND MEDIA COVERAGE' ACTION PLAN?

It is conceptualized to do the LIVE Broadcast of all the 9 Golf Days plus the Launch Party and Closing Ceremony. Launch Party and Closing Ceremony shall be in a five-star hotel. Media coverage in print, on-line, social, magazines, radio, Golf Apps, Mailers etc. shall be extensive.

WHAT IS THE TEAM COMPOSITION AND PLAY FORMAT?
TEAM COMPOSITION

A Team will have a squad of 24 Players, 6 from each category as under. A Team will have a Professional Golfer as Coach for the Team.

HANDICAP INDEX NO. OF PLAYERS
0-4 6
5-8 6
9-11 6
12-14 6

A total of 12 Players will play on a Golf Day, 2-4 from each category depending on format of play for the day.

PLAY FORMAT

The matches will be played under one of the following formats:

  • Individual Match Play
  • Individual Stroke Play
  • Individual Stableford
  • 2 Ball Team - Better Ball Match Play
  • Any other format as decided by Tournament Committee.
